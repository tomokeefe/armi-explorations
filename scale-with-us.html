What We Offer

Scale With Us.

ARMI is the national launchpad for regenerative medicine. You've built something that works in the lab. Now you need the infrastructure, systems, and support to move from prototype to production, faster.

The ReGen Stack

Our full-stack commercialization platform. From prototype to production.

01

Manufacturing Infrastructure

GMP facilities. Closed, automated systems. Real production support. Not promises or PDFs.

02

Regulatory & Reimbursement

IND prep, FDA alignment, payment strategy. Built-in guidance, not guesswork.

03

Startup Scale-up Programs

BioFab Startup Lab. Mentorship, cohort support, and investor visibility.

04

Capital Readiness

Get capital-ready. Investor summits, private briefings, and introductions to aligned funds.

05

Talent & Workforce

Hire from our FDA-ready apprenticeship pipeline, or train your own inside ARMI.

06

Community & Network

200+ member organizations. 10+ federal agencies. ReGen Valley at your back.
