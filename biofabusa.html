BioFabUSA

Manufacturing the ReGen Revolution

BioFabUSA is the advanced manufacturing platform at the heart of ARMI, where therapies move from discovery to scalable production. If you're developing a cell, tissue, or organ-based therapy, here is where discovery becomes real-world ready.

Watch The Video

Built To Deliver

Scale for cell, tissue, and organ-based therapies.

BioFabUSA provides the GMP facilities, automation, and expertise to bring regenerative therapies to scale. Fast. Safe. Repeatable production. Without reinventing the system.

What's Inside BioFabUSA

A full-stack manufacturing environment for building, scaling, and testing regenerative therapies.

01

GMP Cleanrooms

Class 10,000 / ISO 7 suites with validated HVAC and environmental control systems.

02

Automated Tissue Foundry

Closed-system workflows for cell and tissue production, ready for clinical scale-up.

03

Bioreactors & Monitoring

Real-time, non-destructive analytics integrated into process control systems.

04

Modular, Scalable Platforms

Adaptable infrastructure for multiple tissue types and therapeutic formats.

05

Standardized Quality Systems

Built-in QA/QC and batch tracking designed for repeatability and regulatory alignment.

06

On-Site Regulatory Support

FDA-experienced teams to guide you through IND prep, tech transfer, and process validation.

Who It's For

BioFabUSA serves teams ready to build what comes next.
