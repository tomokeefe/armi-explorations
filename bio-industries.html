Bio Industries

Enabling the Bio Industrial Revolution.

ARMI connects companies, researchers, and manufacturers building the next generation of bio-based materials, products, and systems.

What We Enable

Infrastructure and expertise for the bio industrial ecosystem.

01

Bio-Based Materials

From engineered tissues to sustainable biomaterials, we provide the manufacturing backbone.

02

Platform Technologies

Scalable, modular systems for diverse biological manufacturing needs.

03

Industry Partnerships

Connect with 200+ member organizations across biotech, pharma, and government.

The future of manufacturing is biological. ARMI is where that future is being built.
