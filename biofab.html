BioFab

Where Biofabrication Meets Scale.

ARMI's BioFab programs bring together the tools, talent, and technology to manufacture biological products at scale.

BioFab Programs

From startup lab to investment summit, our programs accelerate your path to scale.

01

BioFab Startup Lab

A cohort-based program for seed to Series A founders. Mentorship, infrastructure, and investor visibility.

02

BioFab Investment Summit

Where investors meet production-ready founders. Annual event in Manchester, NH.

03

BioFab Consulting

Tactical execution support for IND prep, process development, quality systems, and scale-up.

Every program is designed to close the gap between breakthrough and bedside.
