ReGen Manufacturing Is A National Imperative.

We help federal agencies and public health leaders responsible for resilience and readiness deliver dual-use readiness for Health and Defense.

If We Can't Scale ReGen Medicine, We Can't Lead it.

Not in health. Not in defense. Not in the global bioeconomy.

That's why the Department of Defense launched ARMI in 2017 to close the gap between discovery and delivery.

What We Deliver

ARMI builds the infrastructure, systems, and workforce needed to manufacture tissues, cells, and organs at scale.

Defense Readiness

Support for warfighter health, battlefield production, and rapid response capabilities across blood, tissue, and advanced therapeutics.

Public Health Access

Scale curative therapies that reduce chronic care costs, expand equity, and strengthen clinical preparedness.

Workforce Development

FDA-ready apprenticeship programs, national EWD network, and advanced biofabrication training programs that serve defense and civilian needs.

Partner on infrastructure, industry standards, and more with over 200 member orgs and 10+ federal agencies

DoD / HHS / BARDA / NIH / NIST / NSF / FDA

"We're not just facing a health crisis, we're facing an infrastructure one."
Jen McDonald, COO, ARMI BioFabUSA
