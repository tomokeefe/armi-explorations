BioFab Consulting

Your Therapy Works. Now Let's Make Sure It Scales.

We help regenerative medicine teams go from lab validation to clinical readiness. Faster, safer, and with fewer surprises.

Schedule a Call

Less Advice. More Execution.

You don't need another advisory board. You need people who've done this before, and can help you do it now.

BioFab Consulting surrounds you with regulatory, process, and scale-up experts to bridge the gap between proof of concept and delivery of products to the clinic.

How We Help

This isn't advisory. It's tactical execution.

01

IND Prep & Strategy

Navigate regulatory milestones with guidance and documentation support from pre-IND to submission.

02

Process Development

Turn your discovery into a closed, reproducible, GMP-ready process.

03

Closed-System Design

Design automated, scalable workflows that reduce cost and contamination risk.

04

Quality Systems

Build QA/QC systems that satisfy FDA expectations and set you up for trial-readiness.

05

Payment Strategy

Clarify your path to coverage, pricing, and long-term viability, before you're in the clinic.

06

Scale-Up Execution

Move from prototype to production with tech transfer, validation, and facility integration support.
